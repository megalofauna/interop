Resizable

Drag-to-resize wrapper with two performance tiers. Pure-CSS resize by default (zero JS in the resize loop); enhancement features — keyboard, snap-to-breakpoints, dimension readout, aspect lock — activate implicitly when their inputs are set.

Native handle

Browser-native resize: both. No pointer listeners, no Angular CD during drag. Drag the bottom-right corner.

Width is also a container-query container — try narrowing me past 320px.

Enhanced handle

Drag the corner or focus it and use the arrow keys. Snaps magnetically near 320 / 480 / 640 / 768px. Hold Shift while dragging to lock aspect ratio, or for a larger keyboard step.

Double-click the handle, or press the button below, to reset.

Aspect lock

Width and height stay in 16:9 throughout the drag.
